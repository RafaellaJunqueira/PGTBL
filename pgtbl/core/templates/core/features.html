{% trans 'Features' %}

  • {% trans 'Manage disciplines and students' %}

    {% blocktrans %} The teacher can add, remove, create and edit courses and classes and provide the password for them to enter. in addition to being able to remove students from the discipline and add it to other disciplines of their own and to manage their grades. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans 'TBL features' %}

    {% blocktrans %} Functions related to the phases of preparation, guarantee of preparation or RAT, iRAT, gRAT and appeals, Application of concepts and evaluation in pairs {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans 'Report' %}

    {% blocktrans %} The teacher will have a dashboard with reports of student performance on each assessment question, feedback to what he or she should focus on most in class, and generate a csv of student proofs with their answers. {% endblocktrans %}

  • {% trans 'Rank and Gamification' %}

    {% blocktrans %} There will also be a group rank in which the first place will be displayed in the Hall of Fame that will be seen by new students of the next semesters, there is no individual rank because the objective is not the competition but the collaboration. In addition to having a gamefication in the list of exercises among other places of the software. {% endblocktrans %}
